Gain Insights

Indoor positioning systems (IPS) are similar to GPS for indoor locations. These systems can detect real-time positions to determine the coordinates of people or assets inside a building using a variety of approaches. To give wayfinding and additional context to users, these coordinates are generally displayed visually by a blue dot on a digital indoor map.
